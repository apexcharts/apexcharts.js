Drag a date range on the top chart to crossfilter both
Both charts share chart.group: 'sales' and opt in with chart.link.enabled. The top chart also has chart.selection.enabled, so dragging a date range on it dims every data point (in both charts) whose date falls outside the range, in place, with no re-render and no axis change. Slide or resize the selection box (or draw a new one) to update the filter live; Clear crossfilter (or chart.clearCrossfilter()) restores. Needs the link feature. Highlight mode works on discrete marks (columns, scatter); a continuous line path is not dimmed per segment.
