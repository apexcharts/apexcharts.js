Zoom into a region (drag on the plot) or hide a series in the legend, then press Save current view: chart.perspectives.capture() plus save(name) stores it. Pick a saved view to apply() it back (the chart animates to that exact state). Copy shareable link uses chart.perspectives.toURL() to encode the view into a #apex= fragment; opening that link restores it on load via ApexCharts.perspectives.fromURL().
