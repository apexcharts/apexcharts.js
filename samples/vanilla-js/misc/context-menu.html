Right-click anywhere on the plot to open the actions menu.
Right-click (or long-press) the plot to open an anchored actions menu. Because each verb receives the clicked data point, it acts right there, not chart-wide like a toolbar button: Add note here drops an editable, draggable callout at the click and opens its floating editor (type to rename; the card also offers accent colors, bold, text size, marker size/shape and delete, via chart.ink). Annotate here drops a dashed vertical line at the clicked x, and Mark this level a dashed horizontal line at the clicked y: always a line, never a rectangle, styled via contextMenu.line (label text, dash pattern, color) and just as draggable, restylable and undoable as the note. A line's editor card has separate Label and Line color rows, so a white label chip never blanks the stroke. Click any note or line later to reopen its editor. Measure from here seeds the ruler at the click, then a second click completes the measurement (chart.measure). The Copy value entry below is a custom item wired via contextMenu.items[].onClick. Arrow keys move, Enter activates, Escape closes.
