Turn on Measure, then drag from A to B on the chart
Click Measure (or hold m) and drag across the plot: a live ruler reads dx, dy, and % change between the two points in data space. Release to pin it; a pinned ruler is stored in data coordinates, so it stays glued to those points when you zoom or resize. Green means the move is up, red means down. Fires the measured event. Needs the overlayCompare feature. Clear removes all rulers.
