Turn on Measure, then drag from A to B on the chart
Click Measure (or hold m) and drag across the plot: a shaded band spans the two points and a readout shows the change, the % change and the range between them, with the endpoints snapped to the price line (the finance-style default, measure.mode: 'span'). Green means the move is up, red means down. Release to pin it; a pinned ruler is stored in data coordinates, so it stays glued when you zoom or resize. Fires the measured event. Set measure.mode: 'free' for a diagonal ruler between any two points. Needs the measure feature. Clear removes all rulers.
