Mean line drawn by the plugin. Press New data to recompute.
The dashed mean line is not part of the chart: it is drawn by a plugin registered with ApexCharts.registerPlugin({ name, setup }) and activated via plugins: [{ name: 'mean-line' }]. On every draw hook the plugin reads api.data, converts the mean to a pixel with api.scales.y(), paints into its own api.layer(), and reports the value back through api.emit(). Press New data: the plugin recomputes and redraws.
