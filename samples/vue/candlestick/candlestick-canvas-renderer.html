The candle bodies and wicks are painted into a single <canvas> while the axes, grid, crosshair, zoom selection, and the OHLC tooltip stay as SVG. Hover to read Open/High/Low/Close, and drag to zoom, exactly as in SVG mode: the y-axis auto-scales to the visible price range on every zoom (via zoom.autoScaleYaxis: true). Note that with renderer: 'auto' candlesticks only promote to canvas at 50,000 candles (they have no per-point markers or data labels for the threshold to count), so this demo selects canvas explicitly. Switch to svg at 50,000+ candles to feel the difference.
