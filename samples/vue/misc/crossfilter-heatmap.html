Click a slice or bar to re-aggregate the heatmap
The heatmap is a 2D cross-tab: its chart.link.dimension returns [day, quarter], so the engine aggregates a day-by-quarter matrix of trade counts. Clicking the outcome donut or the day bar re-aggregates the heatmap over just the matching trades and it recolors in place. (Phase A: the heatmap is a re-aggregating target; clicking a heatmap cell to filter is a later phase.) Reset clears everything.
