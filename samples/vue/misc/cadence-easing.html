Easing:
Pick an easing, then Shuffle data to watch the bars tween to their new heights with that curve. Set it via chart.animations.easing: a registered name ('easeOutBack'), a cubic-bezier [0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1], or a function. Register your own with ApexCharts.registerEasing('bounce', fn). The default, 'easeInOutSine', is the historical curve, so existing charts animate exactly as before. Easing governs the data-update tweens whose tempo is dynamicAnimation.speed (set to 900ms here so the curve reads clearly); the tuned initial-draw pen and pop animations keep their own feel.
