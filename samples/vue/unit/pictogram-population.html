Pictogram (isotype) unit chart

A pictogram is a unit chart whose marks are icons instead of dots. Here each icon stands for a block of people via plotOptions.unit.unitValue (1 icon = 25,000 people), so a large population reads as a countable crowd.

1 icon = 25,000 people
